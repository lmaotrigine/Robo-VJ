Community Rules

In order to maintain a healthy community, please abide by the following rules at all time. This list of rules is by no means exhaustive. If unsure about whether something falls within the rules, please consult a staff member beforehandâ€”we'll be happy to help you out.

General Rules

  1. Follow the Discord Terms of Service and the Discord Community Guidelines.
  2. Follow the Code of Conduct.
  3. Follow and respect staff members and their instructions.
  4. No spamming or unapproved advertising of unrelated content, including other Discord servers, and social media pages.
  5. Keep discussions relevant to channel topics and guidelines.
  6. No selfbot/userbot usage.
  7. English is the primary language of the server. Other language conversations belong in the designated channels.

Quiz Rules

  1. Mute yourself on the bounce voice channel as a rule. Unmute yourself only when you wish to speak to the QM. Alternatively, use push to talk.
  2. Do not Google, or make any other attempts to look up answers online. This warrants an outright ban.
  3. Constructive criticism is always welcome, but that doesn't give you the right to be rude. If you have issues regarding accuracy or ambiguity in any questions, take it up with the QMs in a civil manner. For the duration of the quiz, QM's decision is final in such matters.
  4. If you feel that the QM has not been fair during a quiz, you can bring it to the attention of the moderators after the quiz is concluded, and it will be looked into.

Infractions

We generally follow a two-strike policy for our rules. If you notice someone breaking them, please mention an online Moderator, or DM our Modmail bot and we'll deal with it as soon as we can.

Possible actions we take include:

  • A public verbal warning.
  • A temporary block from specific channels.
  • A permanent block from specific channels.
  • A temporary server-wide mute.
  • A permanent server-wide mute.
  • A kick from the server.
  • A temporary ban from the server.
  • A permanent ban from the server.

While serious infractions are discussed internally before a punishment is given, simpler infractions are dealt with by individual staff members and the punishment they hand out is left to their own discretion.

Don't DM any staff members to appeal a ban. Use this form instead.

Above all, use common sense

Nothing is perfect, that includes our rules list. Any loopholes will not be tolerated. We may moderate anything not on this list that we deem inappropriate. Use your common sense and do not complain when the staff team tries to keep the server a calm and safe environment.
